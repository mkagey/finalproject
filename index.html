Welcome to Downtown Harrisonburg

About

Harrisonburg holds so much! There are over 200 restaurants, a handful of breweries, and a whole lot of fun. However, it can be hard to navigate through everything but not anymore! This website is designed to help you navigate all the wonder that downtown Harrisonburg holds. From food, to shopping, to activities, to places to live, this website is designed to help you successfully do it all! Scroll away!

Activities

There are a plethora of activites in downtown Harrisonburg. There are escape rooms for those who like a challenge and for all those cat lovers there is a cat cafe. Last, but not least, there is Rocktown yarn which is there for all your knitting needs and wants!

Good Eats

Downtown Harrisonburg is filled with resturaunts to fulfill every craving. It has pizza, burgers, sushi, and so much more. The restaurants you find downtown can't be found anywhere else.

Shops

If you still haven't found sommething to do, take a look at all the shops in downtown Harrisonburg. There is a wide range from bike shops to adorable boutique shops.

Need a place to live?

After looking at all the downtown Harrisonburg has to offer you probably want to live here! There are so many great apartments located downtown that have a wide range of accomodations!
